How LifeLink Works

For Donors 🩸

  1. Sign Up - Create account with your phone/email
  2. Verify - Receive OTP verification code
  3. Complete Profile - Add blood type and health info
  4. Browse Requests - View nearby blood requests
  5. Accept Request - Help save a life in your community
  6. Track History - Keep records of all donations

For Hospitals 🏥

  1. Register - Submit hospital credentials
  2. Get Verified - Pass verification within 24 hours
  3. Post Requests - Create urgent blood requests
  4. Track Responses - Receive real-time donor responses
  5. Manage Donors - Communicate with verified donors
  6. Save Records - Maintain secure transaction history

Trust & Safety

Your Safety is Our Priority

🏥 Hospital Verification

Every hospital is manually verified with official registration documents. We confirm licenses and credentials.

🔐 Encrypted Data

Your personal information is never shared publicly. All donor data is encrypted and protected under strict privacy laws.

✅ Donor Screening

Donors complete health questionnaires. Hospitals can verify donor eligibility before accepting donations.

🛡️ Privacy First

Hospitals never get your home address. Direct contact is only after you accept a specific request.
