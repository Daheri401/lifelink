How LifeLink Works

Simple steps to start saving lives

🩸

For Donors

Join our network of lifesavers

  1. 1 Create account with your phone/email
  2. 2 Receive OTP verification code
  3. 3 Add blood type and health info
  4. 4 View nearby blood requests
  5. 5 Accept request and help save a life
  6. 6 Track your donation history
🏥

For Hospitals

Fast access to verified donors

  1. 1 Submit hospital credentials
  2. 2 Pass verification within 24 hours
  3. 3 Create urgent blood requests
  4. 4 Receive real-time donor responses
  5. 5 Communicate with verified donors
  6. 6 Maintain secure transaction records
